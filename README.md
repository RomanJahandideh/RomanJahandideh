# Roman Jahandideh

<img src="github-header.png" alt="Roman Jahandideh banner"/>

Technical Artist with a background in architecture who enjoys building digital worlds instead of buildings. I work with Unity and creative technologies to design interactive environments, prototype immersive experiences, and explore ideas in real-time 3D and spatial computing.

My work focuses on combining **design, technology, and storytelling** to create engaging interactive systems and experimental environments.

Explore more projects and environments on my portfolio:  
https://romanjahandideh.com


## Featured Work

### Environment Design
Real-time architectural environments and spatial experiences designed for interactive media and game engines.

### Unity Tools
Custom Unity workflows, interactive prototypes, and experimental systems exploring real-time simulation and player interaction.

### Shaders
Visual experiments with real-time materials, lighting systems, and shader-based effects for immersive environments.

### Interactive Experiments
Creative technology explorations combining **design, code, and narrative systems** to produce experimental interactive experiences.
